ABOUT THE MUSICIAN

Josh Warmuth is a ten year musician slowly honing in on his perfect sound. In a search to be unique, Josh has begun to focus on a more percussive style of playing. At times it almost sounds as if there is a whole band even though there is just one person. I can't say it's the best show in the world, but you won't leave disappointed :)
